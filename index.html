Study Overview

This portal provides interactive access to RNA-seq data from 6 DIPG patient samples compared to normal neural progenitor cells. Each patient sample includes biological replicates (2-3 per patient), enabling robust statistical analysis of differential gene expression in this aggressive pediatric brain tumor.
